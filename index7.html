Chapter 2 — Capabilities

Everything apparel businesses need, nothing they don't.

Six purpose-built modules designed for the unique demands of fashion, footwear, and apparel brands.

Product Variants

Define colors, sizes, and custom dimensions in a visual matrix. Generate every combination with one click and manage availability across channels.

01 / 06

Seasonality

Organize collections by season with date ranges, delivery windows, and status tracking. Manage the full lifecycle from design to markdown.

02 / 06

Soft Allocations

Reserve inventory before it arrives. Allocate purchase orders to sales orders, customers, or stores with priority-based rules.

03 / 06

Landed Costs

Calculate true product costs including freight, duties, insurance, and handling. Allocate charges across items by weight, value, or quantity.

04 / 06

Product Attributes

Extend items with up to 30 custom attributes per product. Define fabric, fit, wash type, and more with typed, validated fields.

05 / 06

Item Statuses

Control product lifecycle with custom status workflows. Block sales, purchases, or transfers based on item status with automatic enforcement.

Chapter 4 — Getting Started

From install to impact in four steps.

01

Install & Configure

Install Volt Apparel from AppSource directly into your Business Central environment. Configure your apparel setup including variant dimensions and attribute templates.

02

Define Your Catalog

Set up your product catalog with variant matrices, custom attributes, and size scales. Define color palettes and fabric specifications for each collection.

03

Plan Your Seasons

Create seasons with delivery windows and manage the full lifecycle. Allocate purchase orders, set pricing tiers, and plan your go-to-market strategy.

04

Run Your Business

Process orders, manage inventory across variants, track landed costs, and gain complete visibility into your apparel operations with real-time Business Central data.
