Capabilities

Everything apparel businesses need,
nothing they don't.

Purpose-built modules that speak the language of fashion, from variant matrices to landed cost calculations.

Product Variants

Define products with multi-dimensional variant matrices. Manage color, size, width, and length combinations with an intuitive grid interface that maps to how apparel teams actually think.

Seasonality

Organize collections by season and delivery windows. Track pre-season orders, in-season performance, and end-of-season markdowns with native BC dimension integration.

Soft Allocations

Reserve inventory for key accounts before stock arrives. Manage priority-based allocation tiers that automatically adjust as purchase orders are received and quantities change.

Landed Costs

Calculate true product costs including freight, duties, insurance, and handling. Distribute charges across shipments by weight, volume, or value with full audit trails.

Product Attributes

Extend items with fashion-specific attributes like fabric composition, care instructions, country of origin, and customs codes. Filter and report across your entire catalog.

Item Statuses

Control product lifecycle from development through discontinuation. Manage status-based workflows that gate purchasing, selling, and transfers based on configurable business rules.
