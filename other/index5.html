Capabilities

Everything apparel businesses need,
nothing they don't.

Six core modules designed with obsessive attention to the workflows that matter most in fashion and apparel.

01

Product Variants

Manage complex color, size, and style combinations through an intuitive variant matrix. Generate all permutations instantly and track inventory at the SKU level with full Business Central integration.

02

Seasonality

Define seasons, delivery windows, and buying periods. Link products to seasons for complete lifecycle tracking from concept through markdown, with full visibility into seasonal performance.

03

Soft Allocations

Reserve inventory for key accounts before goods arrive. Manage allocation priorities, track fulfillment rates, and automatically convert allocations to sales orders when stock is received.

04

Landed Costs

Calculate true product costs including freight, duties, insurance, and handling. Distribute costs across purchase lines using weight, volume, or value methods for accurate margin analysis.

05

Product Attributes

Extend product data with up to 30 custom attributes per item. Define fabric composition, care instructions, origin country, and any apparel-specific data your business requires.

06

Item Statuses

Control product lifecycle with configurable status workflows. Manage items from development through active selling to discontinuation, with automatic enforcement of business rules at each stage.

Process

Four steps to
transform your operations.

From installation to full operation, Volt Apparel integrates seamlessly into your existing Business Central environment.

Step 01

Install & Configure

Deploy Volt Apparel directly from Microsoft AppSource. Configure your apparel-specific settings: define size ranges, color palettes, and season structures that match your business.

Step 02

Define Your Catalog

Build your product catalog using the variant matrix. Set up color/size grids, assign attributes, and generate all SKU combinations. Link items to seasons and delivery windows.

Step 03

Plan Your Seasons

Create buying plans, set allocation priorities, and manage purchase orders. Track incoming inventory against customer commitments with full visibility across your supply chain.

Step 04

Run Your Business

Process sales orders, manage fulfillment, track landed costs, and analyze performance. Everything you need, running natively inside the Business Central you already know.
