Core Capabilities

Everything apparel businesses need,
nothing they don't.

Purpose-built modules that extend Business Central with deep apparel industry functionality. No bloat, no workarounds.

Product Variants
Auto-generate every color-size combination with semantic variant codes. Define your matrix once, and the system creates all SKUs instantly with consistent naming conventions across your entire catalog.
Seasonality
Organize collections into seasons with delivery groups. Track buy plans, manage pre-season orders, and maintain clear visibility across seasonal timelines.
Soft Allocations
Match demand to supply with priority-based sequencing. Allocate incoming inventory to sales orders before goods arrive, ensuring your best customers get served first.
Landed Costs
Automatic cost accruals at receipt with HTS code integration. Calculate true product costs including freight, duty, insurance, and handling without manual journal entries.
Product Attributes
30 custom attribute slots per item for fabric, weight, composition, care instructions, and more.
Item Statuses
Lifecycle control with 7 blocking operations. Manage items from development through discontinuation with granular control.
Setup Wizard
Guided configuration that gets you running in minutes, not days. No implementation consultants required for the basics.
