Core Modules

Everything apparel businesses need,
nothing they don't.

Six purpose-built modules that transform Business Central into a complete apparel ERP platform.

Product Variants

Size/color variant matrices with automatic SKU generation. Manage thousands of variants effortlessly across your entire catalog.

Seasonality

Plan and manage seasonal collections with delivery windows, drop dates, and lifecycle tracking from concept to markdown.

Soft Allocations

Reserve incoming inventory across channels before goods arrive. Priority-based allocation ensures your best customers get stock first.

Landed Costs

Calculate true product costs including freight, duties, insurance, and handling. Multi-currency support with automatic cost allocation.

Product Attributes

Flexible attribute system for fabric, fit, wash, and custom properties. Filter, search, and report on any attribute combination.

Item Statuses

Lifecycle management from development through active selling to discontinuation. Control purchasing, sales, and transfers by status.
