The craft behind the cost

Track freight, duties, insurance, and handling charges with full transparency. Allocate costs across purchase lines with configurable methods. Know the true cost of every garment from factory floor to fitting room.

Thirty dimensions of detail

Capture every nuance that defines a product: fabric weight, wash type, fit profile, sustainability certifications, care instructions, and more. Rich attribute data powers search, filtering, and reporting across your entire catalogue.

Four movements
