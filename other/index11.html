The Collection

Everything apparel businesses need, nothing they don't.

Six meticulously crafted modules designed for the rhythm of fashion. From concept to closeout, Volt Apparel understands how apparel moves.

01

Every color.
Every size.
One click.

Product Variant Matrices let you define every combination of color, size, and style in a single elegant interface. Generate hundreds of SKUs in seconds, not hours.

Product Variants
02

From runway to retail,
season by season.

Organize your catalog by season, collection, and delivery window. Track sell-through and manage markdown calendars with the precision your business demands.

Seasonality
03

The right pieces,
to the right stores.

Soft allocations let you plan distribution before inventory arrives. Reserve stock by location, channel, or customer group, then convert to hard allocations when you are ready.

Soft Allocations
04

Know your true cost,
from factory to floor.

Capture duties, freight, insurance, and handling charges at the shipment level. Landed cost calculations flow automatically into your item costs for accurate margins.

Landed Costs
05

30 ways to define
every piece.

Rich product attributes capture everything from fabric composition and care instructions to fit type and country of origin. Filter, search, and report on any dimension.

Product Attributes
06

From concept
to closeout,
controlled.

Item lifecycle statuses govern what actions are available at each stage. Control purchasing, sales, and transfers with configurable status rules that protect your process.

Item Statuses
