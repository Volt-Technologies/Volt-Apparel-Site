How It
Works.

Four steps from installation to running your apparel business on Business Central.

01

Install & Configure

Install Volt Apparel from Microsoft AppSource. Configure seasons, size scales, color palettes, and cost allocation rules to match your business.

02

Define Your Catalog

Create styles with variant matrices. Assign attributes, set cost structures, and organize products into seasonal collections.

03

Plan Your Seasons

Build purchase orders, set delivery windows, and allocate incoming inventory to your channel partners and retail locations.

Run Your Business

Process orders, track shipments, analyze sell-through, and manage markdowns. All within the Business Central environment you already know.
