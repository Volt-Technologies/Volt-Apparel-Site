Core Capabilities

Thoughtfully Built

Six modules designed with the same care and intentionality you bring to your collections. Every detail considered, every workflow refined.

Product Variants

Every combination, beautifully organized

Manage size-color matrices with intuitive variant generation. Define hierarchies across dimensions, create bulk variants in seconds, and maintain clarity across thousands of SKUs. The complexity stays behind the scenes.

Seasonality

Seasonal rhythm, naturally managed

Align your operations with fashion's natural cadence. Define seasons, manage delivery windows, track collection performance, and plan transitions. Your calendar, your rhythm, your way.

Soft Allocations

Thoughtful distribution, zero waste

Reserve inventory intelligently before orders are confirmed. Distribute across channels with real-time visibility, prevent overselling, and honor commitments. Allocation should feel effortless.

Landed Costs

Transparent costs, from source to shelf

Capture every cost component from factory floor to retail shelf. Duties, freight, insurance, handling - all allocated with precision. True cost transparency for better margins and honest pricing.

Product Attributes

30 attributes that tell the full story

Capture the richness of every product. Fabric composition, care instructions, sustainability certifications, origin - over 30 attributes that tell the complete story of your garments.

Item Statuses

Mindful lifecycle management

From concept through production, selling, markdown, and archive. Track every product's journey with clear status transitions that keep your entire team aligned and your operations flowing.
